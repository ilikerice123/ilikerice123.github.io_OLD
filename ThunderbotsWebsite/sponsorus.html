As a sponsor, you have the unique opportunity to empower a diverse group of student talent. Thunderbots selects top UBC students in order to compete on an international stage each year at RoboCup International. The focus moving forward is the design of an entirely new fleet of robots that will allow for greater accuracy and improved robustness. With your help we will be able to achieve this goal.

As a member of our sponsor community your logo will also be displayed on at our competition and on our social media (dependent on the level of sponsorship). Competition units around 3000 participants from 40 countries all interested in futures in robotics and AI. Further details can be found in the Sponsorship Package.

Contact robocup@ece.ubc.ca if you have any questions about this opportunity.
